Chicken Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  2. Add chicken and stir-fry until no longer pink.
  3. Remove chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. Add garlic and ginger to the skillet and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  5. Add vegetables and stir-fry until they are crisp-tender.
  6. Return the chicken to the skillet and stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce.
  7. Stir-fry for an additional 2 minutes.
  8. Serve hot over rice or noodles.
